Concept & Inspiration

My work is centered around the idea of the eternal flower — a bloom that quietly holds time, memory, and touch.

Why flowers?

Flowers mark moments of care and celebration. They bloom briefly, then disappear. By recreating them in fabric, I allow them to exist beyond nature’s cycle of fading.

Why ribbons?

Ribbons carry softness and movement. Shaped into petals, they become a gentle language — expressing femininity, intimacy, and quiet strength.

Thematic Essence

Weaving together memory, sustainability, and identity. By transforming textile materials, I create enduring forms that reflect care and a deep connection to nature.
